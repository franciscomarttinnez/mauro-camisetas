Mauro Camisetas – Official Website

Responsive football jersey catalog website built to showcase products, store information, and contact options for customers in Argentina.

Project Overview

Mauro Camisetas is a clean and modern landing page designed as an online catalog for a football clothing store located in Resistencia, Chaco. The website highlights the main product categories, provides store location and opening hours, and makes it easy for users to check availability and contact the business through social media.

The main goal of this site is to offer a simple browsing experience where visitors can explore what the store offers and quickly access the online catalog or reach out directly via Instagram or WhatsApp.

Main Features

✅ Product sections with clear categories (Jerseys, National Teams, Sportswear)

✅ Store location and business hours displayed in a dedicated section

✅ Shipping information (nationwide delivery across Argentina via Andreani)

✅ “News & Promotions” section linked to Instagram updates

✅ Quick access button to open the full online catalog

✅ Contact section to ask for prices, sizes, and stock availability

✅ Responsive layout for desktop and mobile devices
